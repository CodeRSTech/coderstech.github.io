### 1. Pong AI: Genetic Algorithms & Neural Networks from Scratch
**The Problem:** Most AI tutorials rely on high-level frameworks like TensorFlow, which abstract away the underlying mathematics. I wanted to understand the "first principles" of machine learning.
**The Hack:** I built a multi-layer feed-forward neural network and a genetic algorithm entirely from scratch using only Python and NumPy. The AI successfully learned to play Pong by evolving its own weights and biases over multiple generations through selection, crossover, and mutation.
**The Tech Stack:** Python, NumPy, Pygame, Evolutionary Algorithms, Matrix Calculus.

### 3. Firmware Manipulation & Computational Photography
**The Problem:** Early digital cameras lacked advanced High Dynamic Range (HDR) capabilities and manual exposure controls required for astrophotography.
**The Hack:** Bypassed the factory operating system of a Canon Powershot by flashing CHDK custom firmware. Wrote custom Lua scripts to force the mechanical shutter to execute 30-second exposures and capture manual EV brackets (-1, 0, +1) for offline image stitching and tone-mapping.
**The Tech Stack:** Embedded Systems, Lua Scripting, Image Signal Processing (ISP), Optics.
